Our Work

Prachesta's work is driven by a profound commitment to giving back to society and creating a more empowered India. Their impact extends beyond individual support to systemic change:

  1. Sustainable Educational Infrastructure: An overarching objective is to expand activities for wider societal impact by creating sustainable educational infrastructure for the underprivileged. This includes planning projects for sustainable financial and infrastructure support for slum children to ensure their induction and continuity in the formal education system.
  2. Through Education: Prachesta firmly believes that education is the key to empowerment. Pratiksha's success, for example, has encouraged and inspired her entire community, re-emphasising the belief about empowerment through education. The organisation aligns with initiatives like "Beti Padhao Beti Bachao" to empower the girl child.
  3. Support: By supporting over 1000 destitute lives and aiding families during crises, Prachesta embodies the spirit of empathy and support, building stronger, more resilient communities.
  4. Future Vision: Prachesta is enthusiastic about undertaking many more projects, leveraging the support of various companies through their CSR funds to continue making a meaningful impact on skill development and academic enhancement within communities. They strive for a world where every child's potential is nurtured, every dream is supported, and every future is empowered, continuing to break barriers and pave the way for a future where education knows no boundaries.
