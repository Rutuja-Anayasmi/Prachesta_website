Empowering Education and Opportunity for Every Child

Our CSR initiatives are dedicated to creating inclusive, safe, and resource-rich environments that empower communities. By supporting education, skill development, and essential infrastructure, we strive to uplift underprivileged children and provide them with opportunities to learn, grow, and realize their full potential.

Our Work

  • 1. Construction of a multipurpose hall to be used for training of blind children for art and culture at Pragati Andh Vidyalaya, Badlapur
  • Objectives :
  • The hall is meant to provide dedicated space for children for their study, training in art, craft and sports.
  • Harness the skills of the children in their respective fields.
  • Project date : April, 2023 - September, 2023
    CSR Sponsors : Solar Turbines India Private Limited

  • 2. Design, construction and set up of Science Laboratory for school students at Anugrah Vidya Mandir run by Anugrah Seva Sadan, Ambernath
  • Objectives :
  • Design, construction and set up of Science Laboratory for school students at Anugrah Vidya Mandir run by Anugrah Seva Sadan, Ambernath.
  • Develop interest of the students in Science subjects.
  • Enhance students’ capabilities and help harness their true potential.
  • Project date : April, 2023 - October, 2023
    CSR Sponsors : Solar Turbines India Private Limited
  • 3. Promote education for girl students- Sponsor the annual education related expenses of 30 poor girl students
  • Objectives :
  • To ensure continuity of education of the girl inmates to enable them complete education and be self sufficient.
  • While Anugrah has taken up the responsibility of providing accommodation and food of these girl inmates, the education expenses of these students need to be supported to reduce the financial burden Anugrah and thus ensure continuity of their education.
  • Project date : April, 2023 - Ongoing (Annual)
    CSR Sponsors : Matrix Freight Systems Private Limited
  • 4. Operation of a pre school and day care center for 50 slum children residing near Panvel station atTree of Life Foundation, Panvel
  • Objectives :
  • The objective of the center is to create an ecosystem for children coming from a completely vulnerable strata of society.
  • The center not only provides pre primary education and day care to the children as their mothers’ work for livelihood.
  • Project date : May, 2025 - Ongoing (Annual)
    CSR Sponsors : Equifax Credit Information Services Pvt Ltd
