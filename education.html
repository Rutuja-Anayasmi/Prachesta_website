Empowering Through Education

Education is the foundation for a brighter future. At Prachesta, we go beyond scholarships to ensure that every child has access to learning, mentoring, and essential resources.

Our Approach

  • Comprehensive Educational Support : We distribute school supplies, uniforms, learning materials, and tuition assistance to students at risk of dropping out.
  • Technology & Skills Development : Our digital literacy initiatives equip students in underserved areas with modern tools and skills.
  • Nurturing Talents : From sports to arts, we encourage students from disadvantaged backgrounds to explore their potential.

Accountability & Transparency

  • Ongoing Expenditure – Tree of Life, Panvel : Every rupee is monitored closely through regular office visits, surprise inspections, and quality checks on services like food and supplies.
  • Verified Implementation : We ensure that support reaches those in need through systematic verification processes.
  • Quarterly Reporting : Our detailed reports are shared with corporate partners, individual donors, and CSR supporters to reflect how funds are used and the outcomes achieved.
  • Authenticity at Our Core : Even during years with limited funding, we remain steadfast in delivering results—earning the confidence of donors and partners alike.

Our Few Success Stories

1. Ankita's Journey to Nursing
Ankita grew up in West Bengal as the daughter of a cycle rickshaw repair mechanic. Despite the many struggles her family faced, she remained determined to continue her education and pursue her dream of becoming a healthcare professional. Step by step, she overcame challenges that might have discouraged many others. Today, she is in her final year of nursing graduation, preparing to serve patients with compassion. Her journey shows that with determination and the right opportunities, dreams can indeed become reality.

2. Pratiksha's Historic Achievement
From a tribal family in Ambernath with parents working as daily wage laborers, Pratiksha grew up surrounded by many limitations. Still, she was determined to change her path through education. Breaking barriers, she became the first in her family to complete secondary schooling and is now pursuing a diploma course at ITI. Her achievement is not only a personal milestone but also a source of inspiration for many young girls in her community who now believe that education can open doors to new possibilities.

3. Piyali's Journey to Academia
Piyali Das grew up in Badarpur, Assam, as the daughter of a small Ayurvedic shop owner. Despite financial struggles and limited academic support, she remained determined to continue her education. With support from Prachesta since 2019, she excelled—scoring 82% in Madhyamik and 76% in Higher Secondary, earning a scooty from the Assam Government. Now in her 5th semester of Zoology Honours at Nabinchandra College, Piyali dreams of becoming a professor to uplift her family and contribute to society. Beyond academics, she is also a talented painter. Her journey is a testament to resilience, talent, and the power of timely support.
