PRACHESTA

From Sanskrit roots meaning "effort" and "endeavor" - Prachesta embodies the relentless pursuit of a noble cause. Born from the belief that every sincere effort, no matter how small, creates ripples of transformation that can change the world.

When six working professionals came together in 2017, they carried more than just a vision—they carried Prachesta, the unwavering commitment to turn possibility into reality. In education, they found their calling. In every underprivileged child, they saw infinite potential waiting to bloom.

"Where there is sincere effort, there is hope. Where there is hope, there is transformation."

Our Mission

Supporting access to education through direct intervention and improving infrastructure for the under-privileged

Our Vision

Education of the under-privileged - Towards a more empowered India

Education

Ensure under-privileged are not deprived of education - their constitution on rights

Community Welfare Program

We support marginalized communities by distributing food, clothes, medicines, and hygiene kits, and by providing relief during natural disasters and crises.

Access To Right Infrastructure

Deserving students should have access to right resource through library, science labs, classrooms, digital and other incidental infrastructure

Our Causes

Provide integrated services for local communities.

Access To Education

We work tirelessly to protect disadvantaged children from exploitation, abuse, and neglect by raising awareness, and providing support to help them thrive in a safe environment.

Build Educational Infrastructure

We support the creation and maintenance of schools, libraries, sanitation, and other essential facilities in rural regions. Education thrives when students learn in a safe and supportive environment.

Bringing Smiles To Our Future

In rural and tribal communities, we find and develop unexplored talents in sports, the arts, and culture. Giving them a stage on which to shine and proudly preserve their heritage is our aim.

Helping Destitute 

Our goal is to improve health outcomes in underdeveloped regions through initiatives like awareness campaigns. Our efforts guarantee that those most in need of basic healthcare receive it.
