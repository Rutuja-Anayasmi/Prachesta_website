Caring Through Community Welfare Programme

Community welfare is about standing with those who need support the most. At Prachesta, we respond to emergencies, provide essential care, and offer long-term support to uplift vulnerable populations.

Our Initiatives

  • Relief Efforts : We provide food packets, medicines, hygiene kits, and clothes to families affected by disasters like cyclones and pandemics.
  • Medical Support : Our health camps and medical campaigns ensure access to preventive care and emergency services.
  • Grassroots Assistance : We support orphanages and assist over 1,000 destitute lives, including 400 families during crises, providing dignity, shelter, and care.

Trust Through Action

  • Verified Distributions : We ensure that supplies reach those in need through thorough checks and monitoring.
  • Impact Reporting : Detailed reports help donors understand how their support translates into real-world outcomes.
  • Stories That Inspire : Before and after accounts highlight how families and communities recover, rebuild, and flourish with collective support.
