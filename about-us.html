Who We Are

Prachesta, an organisation signifying hope and optimism for the future generation, is a registered Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) dedicated to educational support for underprivileged children across various geographies in India. Established in 2017 in Maharashtra,

Prachesta emerged from a recognition of inequity in education, aiming to bridge the gap and provide an equal chance for all. The journey since its inception has been described as a "roller coaster ride," enduring trials and tribulations while remaining steadfast in its mission.


Our Core Values / Beliefs

At Prachesta, our work is guided by a set of core values that reflect our unwavering commitment to social justice, dignity, and equal opportunity. These principles shape every initiative we take and every life we touch.

Access to Education

Education is a right, not a privilege. We ensure no child is denied learning due to financial barriers.

Equality

We stand for equal opportunity for all—regardless of caste, gender, or religion. Everyone deserves a fair chance.

Dignity for All

We treat every individual with respect and compassion—empowering them to live with pride and purpose.

Community Empowerment

Sustainable change begins locally. We work with communities to identify needs and drive long-term development.

Achievements Through the Years

2017

Prachesta started its mission to transform education for underprivileged children.

2018

Provided educational assistance to 300+ children across Maharashtra, West Bengal, and Assam.

2019

Achieved a 95% academic success rate with noticeable improvement in scholastic performance.

2020

Sponsored 40 tribal children from Ambernath, covering annual education and living expenses.

2021

Built a Multipurpose Training Hall for blind students in Badlapur and a Science Lab in Ambernath.

2022

Supported a tribal girl who became the first in her community to pass SSC, ITI, and secured employment.

2023

Celebrating 7 years of continuous commitment to educational transformation.
